Spiderman

In the iconic Spiderman movie, Uncle Ben imparts his wisdom to Peter Parker: "With great power comes great responsibility." This powerful message has become a cornerstone of Spider Man's character development in various adaptations. Embrace the responsibility that comes with your abilities. - Spiderman Movie
